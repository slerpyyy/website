New Website!

March 28, 2023 · 2 min

After not looking after my website for over 2 years, I finally decided to redo it entirely and turn the domain into a blog.

As I'm writing this, the new website is still kinda work in progress, so in case you want to visit the old one, there should be a "legacy" button in the top right corner1.

For the past couple of years, I've been making cool stuff and posting about it here and there, but I didn't have one place to document it all. Some of my projects are on Github, most of my demoscene productions are only on pouët and Demozoo, other things I only briefly talked about on Twitter or Mastodon, and there are entire write-ups I just dumped into Discord channels or other online forums.

This blog is going to be a collection of "Stuff I Made". This might be write-ups of projects I worked on, post-mortems of past demoscene productions, or other programming, computer science, or math-related stuff.

Also, many of the posts here are going to be archives of things I originally posted somewhere else (e.g. Twitter threads, forum posts). In those cases, the date on the post will be set to show when the original was posted. This right here is the first post with the correct date.

Anyway, that's it for now, thank you.
